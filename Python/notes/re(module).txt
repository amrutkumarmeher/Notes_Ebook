regex is a module that allows us  to search any regular pattern in a string.

Func:
>.search(str(for search),str(document))
it will search for any regular pattern(str for search) in document.

>.findall(pattern,str(document))
it will serch and collect(in list) matched strings that they got in document.

>.compile(pattern)
it will allow you to store your patter in a varable and reuse it.
